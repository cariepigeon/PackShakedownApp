Virtual Pack Shakedown

Are you an upcoming thru hiker who wants a quick pack shakedown before you start your big adventure? Try the virtual pack shakedown tool! Simply click on the weight 'type' you want to use to weigh your base weight. Then click on the button of the item you plan on loading up in your pack, then enter the weight for that item.
